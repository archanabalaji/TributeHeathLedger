Heath Ledger

"The man who brought life to The Dark Knight"

Heath Ledger was an Academy Award-winning, Australian actor best known for his roles in Brokeback Mountain and The Dark Knight.Heath Ledger was born on April 4, 1979, in Perth, Australia. His breakout role was in the film 10 Things I Hate About You with Julia Stiles.Ledger received "Best Actor of 2005" awards from both the New York Film Critics Circle and the San Francisco Film Critics Circle for his performance in Brokeback Mountain. He later received Academy Award and Golden Globe nominations for his role in the same movie.He received a posthumous Best Actor Academy Award for his performance as "The Joker" in the movie "The Dark Knight".
